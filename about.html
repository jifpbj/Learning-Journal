How I stay committed to learning

After stepping away from the entertainment art world, I needed something that felt mine again. Coding gave me that spark—I stay committed by building small things that excite me, reflecting often, and letting curiosity lead the way, one line of code at a time.

How I got started

It began with a single line: “Hello, world.” I didn’t know much, but I started documenting every concept I learned, rewriting it in my own words, and turning ideas into tiny, personal projects. Sharing those notes helped me stay motivated—and made me feel like I wasn’t learning in isolation.
